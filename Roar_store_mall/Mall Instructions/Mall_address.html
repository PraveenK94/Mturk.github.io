INSTRUCTION TO FETCH MALL ADDRESS

1. Navigate to official website and go to either About Us or Contact Us page to get the address of the mall (You may have to look through multiple pages on the mall website to find the address if its’s not listed under About Us or Contact Us pages)..

 
 

Filling Address line 1 and 2 & Filling Postal Code – ( Incorrect capturing of Address line 1 and 2 would result in lower quality)

a) Capture street number & name in Address line 1 field. In the above example for King of Prussia- 160 N Gulph Rd, King of Prussia would be captured in Address line 1

b) Capture the suite number or store number or building number in ‘address line 2’.In the above example there is no suite/building/store number hence Address line 2 be left blank. If no suite number is provided, then ‘address line 2’ can be left blank.

There is no need of capturing City/ State / Zipcode in Address line 1 & 2.

While collecting the Address lines from official store website " Capturing of city name , state name and zipcode is not required", collect only the street names in address line 1 and suite numbers in "address line 2". For ex ("6800 OXON HILL ROAD SPC 855" , consider "6800 OXON HILL ROAD - address line 1" & "SPC 855 - address line 2" and address line 3 can be left blank. In few cases the address line 2 will be like this "SPC 800"/ "STE E"/"BLDG 10"/"Unit 20", also do not consider the range number which is provided before the street name as "suite number".

More examples on how to capture address-

1) 6909 N. LOOP 1604 EAST #2065
SAN ANTONIO, TX 78247
Here Address line 1- 6909 N. LOOP 1604 EAST , Address line 2 - #2065

2) 1213 E TRINITY MILLS RD RM 700
CARROLLTON, TX 75006
Here Address line 1- 1213 E TRINITY MILLS RD , Address line 2 - RM700
