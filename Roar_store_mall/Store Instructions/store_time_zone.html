INSTRUCTIONS FOR FETCHING TIME ZONE

a) Google “Time zone for City and State” of the store. Example “Time Zone for Westminster IN Maryland”

 

b) Once you have the google result, use the result to find the time zone in the below provided table.

Google Result Time Zone
Central Standard Time /Central Time Zone/Central Daylight Time America/Chicago
Eastern Standard Time/ Eastern Daylight Time America/New_York
Alaska Standard Time America/Anchorage
Pacific Standard Time/Pacific Daylight Time America/Los_Angeles
Mountain Standard Time/ Mountain Daylight Time America/Denver
 

c) For example – Time zone for City King of Prussia and State PA is “Eastern” time zone in google.

d) Find for "Eastern" in the table above and collect the results. (Ex: When you look for Eastern in the table, collect the available time zone which is “America/Newyork”, Select "America/Newyork" from the dropdown options.

Note - If the store is in the state Arizona (AZ) the time zone is always "America/Phoenix", for store in the state Hawaii (HI) the time zone is always “Pacific/Honolulu” and for store in the state Puerto Rico (PR) the time zone is always “America/Puerto_Rico”.
